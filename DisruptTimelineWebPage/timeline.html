ROUND DETAILS
ROUND 0: INITIAL SCREENING
The Registered Teams will fill out a Questionnaire about the details of their startup, such as PS, proposed solutions, and their current stage. 
ROUND 1:
The top 10 Startups for each track will be selected for Round 1. In Round 1 of the competition, participating teams will be required to complete tasks corresponding to their current startup stage, with submissions aligned accordingly to their respective stage.
ROUND 2: STAGE ADVANCEMENT ROUND
Now that the teams have completed their current stage in Round 1, Round 2 teams will be assigned tasks corresponding to the next stage of the startup, with submissions aligned accordingly to their respective next stage.
D-DAY: FINAL PITCH
The top 2-3 Startups from each track will be selected to pitch their idea to the panel of judges, VCs, and angel investors.
FAQ
What is Disrupt?
Disrupt is a Startup Bootcamp—a revolutionary platform empowering aspiring entrepreneurs across India. This immersive program offers a tailored experience to shape startup ideas, develop minimum viable products (MVPs), and fuel exponential growth.
I want to participate in DISRUPT 2023. What are the steps I should follow?
Familiarize yourself with what DISRUPT 2023 is all about .On the official DISRUPT 2023 website find the registration section and follow the instructions provided to sign up.
What is the registration/ participation fee for DISRUPT ?
The registration and participation for DISRUPT do not incur any fees. It's a cost-free opportunity to engage and be a part of the event.
Can I sign up for multiple tracks among DISRUPT ?
Yes , you're welcome to express interest in multiple tracks during your sign-up process. However, after a thoughtful evaluation your startup will be thoughtfully placed in the track that best matches its nature and potential .
