ACHIEVEMENTS

Over the years, E-Cell, IIT Guwahati has been diving into the field of entrepreneurship, innovation and development; and has attained several accomplishments in varied sectors.
The dedication, enthusiasm and endeavour of the team led to this rewarding journey of bagging achievements.
Best Technical Club (2018-19)
Earned the “Best Technical Club” award in the year 2018-19 amongst 12 technical clubs in IIT Guwahati.
Inter IIT Tech Meet 8.0
Won Gold in the Inter IIT Tech Meet 8.0 conducted by IIT Roorkee. A “Smart Dustbin” was proposed for the problem statement “Social Innovations.”
Funding through Disrupt
Edvizo, a IITG alumni startup, received a $150K funding from IP Ventures, judges of Disrupt, a pitching battle conducted under UDGAM (the annual E-summit of IIT Guwahati).
PMx - Product Management Expedition
UDGAM hosts various events, one of which is PMx, India’s second-largest Product Management competition. With over 1200+ registrations from around the country and around 450+ members in the Slack Community, PMx was a massive success in its 2nd edition (UDGAM 2021).
Flyzy
Flyzy, a startup by E-Cell alumni - Deepak Meena and Hansraj Patel, has been recognised by Startup India & Ministry of Education and has been the finalist at startup India COVID-19 Challenge.
GEB
Two E-Cell IITG alumni Deepak Meena and Hansraj Patel, got selected for the Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp, which was held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
PM Live, IIM Ahmedabad
E-Cell Alumni Aniket Mandle and Utkarsh Mishra, emerged as top 10 finalists in IIM Ahmedabad’s competition PM Live that witnessed participation from 1500+ participants. They were the only UG students team in the top 10.
LensHood
LensHood, a startup by Bhargava Yalamati and E-Cell alumni - Mukesh Babu, got selected to attend the MIT Innovation and Entrepreneurship Bootcamp.
