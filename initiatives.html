Startup Odyssey
Startup Odyssey 2022


Have a dream of starting up?
Who can be a better teacher and guide than those who have successfully started up and on the way to success have experiences of failures? We at E-Cell IIT Guwahati care for your entrepreneurial dreams.
Under Startup Odyssey, we organize lectures and talks with some of the most successful stalwart entrepreneur leaders who have led startups to success and have a great entrepreneurial vision. It is a very comprehensive venture that aims to cover every aspect of startups. Be it a question like “should I startup?” or a solution to the problems faced at an advanced stage, after starting up, we will organize events answering all these problems.




UDGAM
UDGAM


UDGAM, the annual E-Summit of IIT Guwahati, is the flagship event of E-Cell, IIT Guwahati. It was conceptualized to develop and spread the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth of the North-East India and India in general.
Its expansion over the years has propelled it to become the North East India’s largest E-summit.
Udgam acts as an exclusive platform for people interested in Startup culture to meet, discuss, explore, share, debate, and have fun, geared towards celebrating the idea of entrepreneurship.




Startup Hive
Startup Hive


“It’s not about Ideas. It’s about making ideas happen.” -Scott Belsky.
We, at E-Cell, IIT Guwahati, are always ready to help you all with your entrepreneurship ideas. Startup hive is a community of IITG campus startups where you could share your work, interact, ideate, exchange thoughts, get help and provide help to one another.
We help in getting mentorship oppurtunities for IITG startups and to equip you with the resources needed. We also share the story of successful startups on various social media, which serves as a motivation for all aspiring entrepreneurs and ensures a greater reach. We also have a glorious past of having many successful startups from our community. So, do reach out for us to be a part of our community.
PM Community
PM Community


PM community is a one-stop platform for future aspirants in the field of product management. The community provides us with a learning platform to hone the skills required to shine in this competitive domain. Within the community, people have been informed about the newly available job and internship opportunities every week.
The PM community gives you a chance to interact with experienced PMs, APMs, and shining interns of this field through AMA and live sessions.
The whole knowledge collected in the community is used to battle down ideas in ‘PMx - a product management case study competition,’ held in Udgam.

Scavenger Hunt
Scavenger Hunt


It is a treasure hunt conducted by E-cell IIT Guwahati, is one of the most memorable and anticipated events of freshman year.
