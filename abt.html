OUR VISION


At E-Cell, IIT Guwahati, we understand and believe that this fast changing and evolving era is the perfect time to live in and step-up to shape our future by looking at the world as our playground!

With a vision to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the IITG community and youth of the North-East India, we host various initiatives providing a complete learning experience to the attendees and filling them with the zeal to change their monotonous lives!
